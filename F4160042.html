Do you use the same password for all personal accounts?
▼
Do you use the same password for all personal accounts?
Why this matters
Using the same password across accounts means one breach can compromise everything. Unique passwords reduce this risk.
Do you use a password manager to store your passwords?
▼
Do you use a password manager to store your passwords?
Why this matters
Password managers make it easier to use strong, unique passwords without needing to remember them all.

▼
Do you use two-factor authentication (2FA) on your important accounts?
Why this matters
2FA adds an extra layer of security, making it harder for attackers to access your accounts even if they get your password.
Do you regularly update or change your passwords?
▼
Do you regularly update or change your passwords?
Why this matters
Regularly changing passwords helps protect against long-term compromise if a password was exposed without your knowledge.

▼
Are any of your passwords short, simple, or reused from old accounts?
